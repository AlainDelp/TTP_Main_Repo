Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, finely diced
  • 1 celery stalk, finely diced
  • 400g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 400g spaghetti
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat some olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion, garlic, carrot, and celery. Saut√© until softened.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, bay leaf, salt, and pepper.
  5. Simmer the sauce on low heat for at least 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  6. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  7. Once cooked, drain the spaghetti and return it to the pot.
  8. Remove the bay leaf from the sauce and pour the sauce over the spaghetti.
  9. Toss the spaghetti until well coated with the sauce.
  10. Serve the spaghetti Bolognese with grated Parmesan cheese on top.
